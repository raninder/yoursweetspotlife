S.T.A.R.T

S.T.A.R.T., or Systematic Training and Restoration Team, offers a comprehensive approach to achieving personal and professional goals. Serving as your dedicated partners, we guide you through every stage of your journey, from ideation to execution. We connect you with opportunities and provide a supportive platform for showcasing and selling your work.

Assess

At the start of our process, we begin with a detailed review to understand the current situation. We look closely at what is going well, where improvements are needed, areas for growth, and potential challenges. This step helps us get a clear picture of where we stand. Our goal is to gather important information that will help guide us through the rest of the process, making sure we offer solutions that are specifically designed to help us reach our goals together.

Plan

After the initial assessment, the journey progresses to the planning stage. In this phase, we develop a strategic plan that is carefully designed using the insights gathered during the assessment. This plan includes clear, measurable goals and objectives, as well as the steps needed to reach these targets. The planning phase acts as the guiding framework for the entire process, setting the path for achieving the outcomes we aim for.

Learn

Once the strategic plan is established, we move into the learning phase of the process. This phase focuses on putting the plan into action through different learning initiatives, such as workshops, training sessions, or community outreach where we can geton the ground insights. The aim here is to gain the essential knowledge and skills they need to effectively carry out the strategic plan.

Grow

Following the learning phase, we enter the growth stage of the journey. This stage is all about observing and encouraging both personal and professional growth. We use key performance indicators (KPIs) to track progress, making sure that everyone involved is improving in their skills, competencies, and general performance.

Create

The process culminates in the creation phase. At this point, we use the knowledge and skills gained to produce concrete results. This is where we create products, services and finalize projects. Reaching the specific goals set out in the original strategic plan. The creation phase signifies the end of the this part of the journey, showcasing the real-world effects and achievements of the process.

Approval Process

Our approval process is integral to ensuring that we select candidates who meet our organization's standards. This process involves conducting interviews through phone, video, or onsite meetings, depending on the candidate's availability. We are looking for the best of the best. companies that create their own products using local, sustainably sourced resources, are innovative and fully dedicated to the communities they serve.

Show Format

Once you are approved we will decide on a filming time in a specific window that works for everyone involved. Due to the cross country nature of the show we only have time for 5 businesses per province. The live show interview will be a 30-minute live segment. We will ask you a set of predetermined questions where: We will talk about your process, challenges and goals for the future. We will show people your business touring through showing some of your processes and then we will hold a short Q&A session for online viewers.

Do I Pay?

In our support for your business journey, we prioritize easing financial burdens. Thus, there are no costs or fees involved in our process. We're committed to providing comprehensive support to help you thrive without financial constraints. Our aim is to empower and assist you as you embark on your business journey, ensuring you have the resources and assistance needed to succeed.

What happens if I don't get accepted?

If you're not accepted through our standard approval process, don't worry—there are still alternative ways for you to get involved. We offer various avenues for participation and engagement within our organization. Whether through volunteering, attending workshops, or exploring other opportunities, we encourage you to stay connected and engaged with us. We value your interest and are committed to finding the right fit for your skills and aspirations.

I'm not a business owner, can I come along?

Yes! We would love to have you. Whether it's to explore this amazing country together or to offer support along the way. Keep an eye out for upcoming packages to make the journey as awesome as you imagine it to be, and feel free to bring your family along for added fun.

Portal Tree Chronicles

"The Portal Tree Chronicles" A metanarrative about a time-traveling prince from another realm who discovers a community of craftsmen. Through his interactions, the artisans' individual tales intertwine, shaping the overarching narrative of their craft's evolution across time. As the story unfolds across different eras and characters, themes of legacy, innovation, and the interconnectedness of human experience weave together to form a captivating tapestry that spans both time and imagination.

What is the approval process?

Businesses that were approved for the YSS Live are automatically approved for the festival. We are looking for more than just vendors and workshop providers here. We are searching for individuals that are passionate about community development and providing long term practical support. We aim to transform the communities we enter.

What is the format of the event?

It is a three day educational and interactive showcase. We are broadcasting across a variety of platforms. We will be showcasing the creation process from workshops on how we extract raw goods, creation stations and educational games. The festival will follow our processes from beginning to end, showcase innovative technologies, storytelling adventures and skills based collision points. .

Do I Need To Pay?

Yes, at this point you do. There is a lot that goes into hosting an event like this. As a festival vendor you will get full access to all our supports. Our CRM, online and in person festival, the S.T.A.R.T. program, mentorship opportunities both as paid mentors and as businesses needing mentorship support themselves. Your payment covers those costs. You also get a fully customized package that fills your needs.
