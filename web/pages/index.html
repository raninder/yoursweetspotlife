Y.S.S Live

10 provinces and three territories coast to coast

Join us on a remarkable journey across Canada, starting in Newfoundland and moving through the Maritime Provinces. We'll head west across the country to British Columbia, then go north to the Yukon, Northwest Territories, and Nunavut, before returning to Labrador. In each place, we'll discover local gems and work with communities to create lasting improvements. We aim to boost local economies, promote tourism, and support community renewal. Our goal is to work together with these communities for sustainable development and a prosperous future for all.

Stage 1: Sponsor us and get involved!

  • Make an Impact: Your support helps us share Canada's diverse stories, support local communities, and promote cultural enrichment.

  • Connect and Grow: Volunteers gain hands-on experience, learn new skills, and connect with communities nationwide.

Stage 2: 4 Pillars (Craftsmanship, Innovation, Education, and Sustainability)

Craftsmanship

During our journey, we'll spotlight regional craftsmanship, blending traditional and modern techniques. Partnering with local artisans, we'll preserve cultural heritage.

Innovation

We'll partner with local communities to find new ideas, like sustainable tech, fresh business models, and creative solutions. This helps grow the economy and environment.

Education

We'll create educational programs in local areas, offering skill development, knowledge sharing, and access to resources. This empowers residents to shape their future.

Sustainability

We'll partner with businesses that prioritize affordable and simple creation processes, ensuring practicality, functionality, and benefits for individuals, families, communities, and the environment in the long term.
